Albert Einstein

Biography

Albert Einstein was a renowned theoretical physicist known for his theory of relativity.

Achievements

He won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his work on the photoelectric effect.

Personal Life

Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany.

Quotes

"Imagination is more important than knowledge."

Legacy

Einstein's work revolutionized our understanding of the universe.

Trivia

He became a U.S. citizen in 1940 and taught at Princeton University.
